
### Business Rules

1.  A user must have a registered account to participate in a hackathon.
2.  A user can have one of the following roles: Participant, Judge, Guest_Judge, Mentor, Organizer, or Admin.
3.  A participant can only be a member of one team per hackathon.
4.  A team must have at least one member to be valid.
5.  Each hackathon must have a start and end date.
6.  Submissions are only accepted during the hackathon's designated submission period.
7.  Each submission is scored by multiple judges to ensure fairness.
8.  The final score for a submission is the average of all scores from the judges.
9.  Organizers cannot participate in their own hackathons.
10. Admins have superuser privileges and can manage all aspects of the system.
